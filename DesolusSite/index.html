About

Desolus is a first person puzzle game created solely by Mark J. Mayers, with music by pianist Kyle Landry.

Harness black holes to solve surreal puzzles, and traverse desolate architecture torn between dimensions.

With reality collapsing, you must embrace the impossible to learn the truth.

Experience Desolus on PC, or also with Virtual Reality.
